# LoopShelf App Icon Prompt

Create a modern app icon for "LoopShelf", a peer-to-peer university textbook rental platform. The icon should show a clean stack of two or three books forming a subtle circular loop or exchange arrow, suggesting books moving between students. Use a trustworthy campus marketplace identity with deep forest green, warm cream, soft mint, and a small golden accent. Style: minimal, polished, friendly, high-contrast vector-like 3D or flat app icon, centered composition, rounded-square background, no text, no tiny details, readable at small mobile icon size.
